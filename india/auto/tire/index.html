Dissecting the Demand

The market is intricately segmented, with demand driven by diverse vehicle types and a dominant replacement market that provides a stable revenue stream.

Market Share by Vehicle Type

The two-wheeler segment commands the largest share, a reflection of its widespread use across urban and rural India. Passenger cars and commercial vehicles also represent significant portions of the market.

Demand: OEM vs. Replacement

The replacement/aftermarket segment is the backbone of the industry, accounting for the majority of revenue and offering resilience against new vehicle sales cycles.

Supply Chain Pressure Points

Raw material dynamics, particularly the significant gap between domestic natural rubber production and consumption, pose a major challenge to the industry's profitability and stability.

Natural Rubber: The Demand-Supply Gap

India is the world's second-largest consumer of natural rubber but only its fifth-largest producer. This structural deficit necessitates heavy reliance on imports, exposing the industry to global price volatility. The chart below visualizes this critical gap for FY2023-24.
